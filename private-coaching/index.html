The Experience

Michi comes to you. Your location, your schedule, your conditions. Whether it's a week in the Maldives, a long weekend in the Mediterranean, or a focused stretch at your home spot - this is coaching built entirely around you.

On the water, Michi coaches you in real time - technique, timing, wave reading, equipment setup. Off the water, daily video analysis breaks down every session frame by frame. Equipment is tuned, progression is tracked, and every detail is dialed in.

But this goes beyond technical. Session planning, mental preparation, and peak performance techniques give you tools that stay with you long after the coaching ends. You'll leave riding differently - and thinking differently about your riding.

The experience begins before Michi arrives. A pre-trip video review of your current riding, a goal-setting conversation, and a custom preparation plan ensure every on-water hour counts. And it doesn't end when the trip does - follow-up sessions keep your progression on track.
