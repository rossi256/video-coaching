Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What level do I need to be for video coaching?
Any level from beginner to advanced. Whether you're working on your first waterstart or trying to land a frontside 360, Michi tailors his feedback to exactly where you are in your progression. The most common riders are intermediate foilers working on jibes, tacks, and transitions.
How does the video coaching process work?
It's simple: you purchase a session, upload your riding clips (any format from your phone or camera), and fill out a quick rider profile. Michi personally reviews your footage, identifies the key issues, and records a detailed video response with frame-by-frame analysis and specific drills. You'll receive your coaching video within 72 hours.
What equipment do I need to film my session?
A smartphone is enough. Ideally, have someone film you from the shore or a boat - side-angle shots are most useful. If you have friends around, let them film you with a GoPro. That would be helpful.
Can I book coaching in person?
Yes! Michi runs group coaching camps at Lake Garda and Tarifa through events.tricktionary.com. For private 1:1 coaching packages and continuous progression subscriptions, these are coming soon - join the waitlist to be notified when they launch.
What's included in the founding price?
The €49 founding price includes unlimited video uploads per session, a personal video response from Michi with frame-by-frame technique breakdown, a custom progression roadmap, and follow-up Q&A.
How long until I get my feedback?
Michi delivers your personal coaching video within 72 hours of receiving your upload. Most responses come within 48 hours. You'll get an email with a private link to your coaching video that you can rewatch anytime.
What makes this different from YouTube tutorials?
YouTube tutorials are generic - they show what perfect technique looks like. Michi's coaching shows you what YOUR technique looks like and exactly what to change. He identifies the specific body position, timing, or equipment issue that's holding you back, and gives you targeted drills to fix it.
Do I need to be in Europe for coaching?
Not for video coaching - it's 100% remote and works from anywhere in the world. For in-person camps and events, Michi is primarily based between Lake Garda (Italy) and Tarifa (Spain). Private coaching trips to other locations can be arranged on request.
